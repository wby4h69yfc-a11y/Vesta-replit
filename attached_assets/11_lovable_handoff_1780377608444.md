  https://feqykyiyqzkmepfeehmg.supabase.co/functions/v1/waitlist-signup

- **Don't let it "modernize" the copy.** Every line was honesty-checked against the product spec (no fake ratings, no overclaimed learning engine, forwarding-not-auto-capture). Verbatim only.
- **Pre-deploy still owed** (same as the static file): real-device render check at 375px, security headers, and — if you ever pasted the Supabase service_role key into Lovable/Replit during prototyping — rotate it.
