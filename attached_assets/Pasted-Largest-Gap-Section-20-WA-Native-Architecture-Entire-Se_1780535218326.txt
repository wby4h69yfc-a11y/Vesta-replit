    Conversation Engine — Multi-turn stateful WA conversations. A user sends a message, Vesta replies in WA with "Confirmar? [Sim] [Editar] [Não]", user replies "sim", action is executed. No app required.

    Proactive Scheduler — Daily digest at 7am, absence alerts, conflict detection, weekly Sunday look-ahead, pattern threshold alerts — all sent as WhatsApp messages, not push notifications (§20.2)
    Conversational memory queries — "Qual o telefone da Dra Marina?" answered directly in WA (§20.4)
    NL Delegation — "Manda pro Pedro confirmar" → Vesta drafts + sends with approval (§20.5)
    Voice transcription pipeline — Audio notes → Whisper/Claude → classified as text (§20.6)
